Daily Checklist

Start of Day

  1. Review due purchase requisitions and open production/project demand.
  2. Validate supplier lead time and stock risk from reorder reports.
  3. Confirm approval status and document numbering policy.

Operations

  1. Create PO and keep references to project_code / item_code / expected date.
  2. Post goods receipt and match quantity and unit cost against PO.
  3. Escalate mismatches and post supplier return if needed.
  4. Coordinate AP billing and payment with Accounting.

Close of Day

  1. Ensure all received documents are posted and not left in draft.
  2. Reconcile open PO vs received vs billed status.
  3. Capture evidence screens for high-value or exceptional transactions.

Audit Evidence To Capture

  1. PO approval screen with supplier and due date.
  2. Goods receipt with item lot/qty.
  3. AP billing and payment posting confirmation.

Use capture_screen.php and tag each screenshot with module_key, process_stage, project_code, run_no, and doc_ref.

Role KPIs

  • PO on-time rate >= target.
  • Receipt variance within approved tolerance.
  • No missing AP document reference at day close.
