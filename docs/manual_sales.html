Daily Checklist

Start of Day

  1. Review customer commitments due today.
  2. Check available-to-promise and pending dispatch from Warehouse.
  3. Prepare open quotations and booking follow-up list.

Operations

  1. Create quotation and booking with clear item, qty, and due date.
  2. Coordinate delivery and confirm client receive document.
  3. Post sale cash/credit document and reference shipment.
  4. Process returns with reason and quantity validation.

Close of Day

  1. Reconcile sales documents against dispatches and bookings.
  2. Confirm AR billing handoff completeness.
  3. Capture evidence for major orders and exceptions.

Audit Evidence To Capture

  1. Quotation-to-booking conversion record.
  2. Delivery confirmation with customer reference.
  3. Sale posting with amount and tax base.
