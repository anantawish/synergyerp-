Daily Checklist

Start of Day

  1. Validate attendance feed and unresolved anomalies.
  2. Check pending leave approvals and policy exceptions.
  3. Confirm current payroll period status.

Operations

  1. Post attendance corrections with clear reason.
  2. Approve/reject leave and update paid/unpaid impact.
  3. Review OT, lateness, and absence deductions.
  4. Run payroll calculation for active period as needed.

Close of Day

  1. Verify attendance, leave, and payroll consistency.
  2. Export HR summary for management and accounting handoff.
  3. Capture compliance evidence for sensitive adjustments.

Audit Evidence To Capture

  1. Attendance correction approval screen.
  2. Leave decision record and balances.
  3. Payroll calculation result by employee.
