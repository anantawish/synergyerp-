Daily Checklist

Start of Day

  1. Verify production orders and APS priorities.
  2. Confirm material availability from Production Warehouse.
  3. Validate approved BOM/routing version before launch.

Operations

  1. Run job sheets per operation and record machine/operator result.
  2. Collect IoT or runtime data for performance and maintenance.
  3. Submit inspection results and isolate failed lots immediately.
  4. Release only PASS lots to Finished Goods warehouse.

Close of Day

  1. Close completed operations and update order status.
  2. Review bottleneck/idle-time and open maintenance risk.
  3. Capture execution timeline evidence by order and lot.

Audit Evidence To Capture

  1. APS board snapshot before dispatch.
  2. Digital job sheet completion and operator sign-off.
  3. QMS PASS/FAIL screen and released lot record.

Role KPIs

  • Plan adherence >= target.
  • OEE/utilization trend up and downtime trend down.
  • First-pass quality rate >= target.
