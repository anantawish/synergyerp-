Role Objective

Control stock movement across Main, Production, Finished Goods, and Packing warehouses with traceable transfers.

Daily Checklist

Start of Day

  1. Check inbound schedule and outbound commitments.
  2. Verify stock discrepancies from prior shift.
  3. Prepare transfer routes: Main -> Production -> FG -> Packing.

Operations

  1. Post all transfers with project_code, lot_no, qty, and warehouse route.
  2. Validate available qty before issue to production.
  3. Receive FG from production and dispatch to packing in sequence.
  4. Confirm shipment issue to Sales/Delivery documents.

Close of Day

  1. Reconcile stock_card against transfer and dispatch records.
  2. Verify no negative stock remains unresolved.
  3. Capture final movement summary screens.

Audit Evidence To Capture

  1. Transfer posting screen for Main -> Production.
  2. FG receipt and FG -> Packing transfer records.
  3. Shipment dispatch linked to customer document.

Use capture_screen.php and tag each screenshot with module_key, process_stage, project_code, run_no, and doc_ref.

Role KPIs

  • Inventory accuracy >= target.
  • Transfer posting latency within shift SLA.
  • Zero untraceable lot movement.
